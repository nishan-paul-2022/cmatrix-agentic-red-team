## 📧 Cold Email Template Guidance

When reaching out, **always open with the specific paper of theirs** that CMatrix directly builds on. Lead with:

1. The paper title + year that sparked your interest in their work
2. CMatrix's live deployment (kaiofficial.xyz) — shows you're not just theoretical

4. A concrete, small ask: "Could CMatrix be evaluated on your [benchmark/dataset]?" or "Would a co-authored survey be of interest?"

**Never** ask for funding in a first email. Build the research relationship first.
