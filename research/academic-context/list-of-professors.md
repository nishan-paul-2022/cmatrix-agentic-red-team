# 📧 Cold Email Strategy

**The golden rule:** Never ask for funding in a first email. Lead with intellectual alignment.

**Template structure:**
1. **Hook** — Name the specific paper of theirs CMatrix directly builds on (e.g., "I read your 2025 CVE-GENIE paper and...")
