## 📌 Notes

- **arXiv links** are always free and don't require a login. Use `https://arxiv.org/pdf/XXXX.XXXXX` for direct PDF.
- **USENIX Security** papers are always open access at `usenix.org`.
- **MDPI** papers are open access — PDFs are directly downloadable.
- **ACM DL** and **IEEE Xplore** may be paywalled unless you have institutional access or there's a linked arXiv preprint.
- For paywalled papers, search the title on **Semantic Scholar** (`semanticscholar.org`) or **Google Scholar** — a free PDF version is often linked.
- **Sci-Hub** (`sci-hub.se`) is another option for paywalled papers if institutional access is unavailable.
