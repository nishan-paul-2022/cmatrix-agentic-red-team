## Strategy Notes

### Cost Management

- **ICCIT** local student rate $55. Virtual — no travel cost.
- **ICCA & ICISET** local BD rates well under $100.
- **PETS** is free for Bangladesh — apply explicitly for GNI waiver at registration.
- **USENIX student grants** — email `student-grants@usenix.org` after acceptance.
- **NDSS Fellowship** (Internet Society) — 24 funded per year. Apply immediately after any acceptance.
- **ACM CCS APC waiver** — available for authors from low-income/middle-income countries. Apply at submission.
- **AAAI / NeurIPS D&I subsidies** — deadline typically 6–8 weeks before conference.

---

### Important Reminders

1. **At least one author must register** at every venue — no exceptions or the paper is removed.
2. **Open Science policy** at USENIX 2026+ requires sharing code/artifacts at submission — CMatrix being open-source is an advantage.
3. **Workshop papers** are real citable publications — don't underestimate them for PhD apps.
4. **arXiv preprint** — post simultaneously with any submission to establish priority and visibility.
5. **"Accepted, forthcoming"** — if a conference result is known before Nov 2027 but proceedings release after, it can still be listed in PhD apps.
6. **UC Berkeley, CMU, MIT, Stanford** weight top-4 security (S&P, CCS, USENIX, NDSS) and top AI (NeurIPS, ICML, ICLR) publications most heavily.
